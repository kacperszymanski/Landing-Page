Coca-Cola

Description:

Coca-Cola is a trademarked brand and recipe for a popular carbonated soft drink. The original recipe for Coca-Cola is a closely guarded trade secret known only by a select few individuals within the company. However, there are a number of "clone" or copycat recipes available online that claim to replicate the taste of Coca-Cola. It's important to note that these recipes may not be exact replicas and that the company does not endorse or support the making of homemade versions of Coca-Cola.
Keep in mind this recipe is not the original and the company does not endorse or support the making of homemade versions of Coca-Cola.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup caramel color
  • 1/2 cup caffeine powder
  • 1/2 cup phosphoric acid
  • 1/4 cup vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup Coca-Cola syrup

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan, combine the sugar, caramel color, caffeine powder, phosphoric acid, vanilla extract, lime juice, and water.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Remove from heat and add the Coca-Cola syrup.
  4. Allow to cool and then transfer to a bottle or pitcher.
  5. To serve, add the mixture to a glass filled with ice and add carbonated water to taste.
